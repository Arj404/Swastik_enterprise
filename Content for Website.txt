Company Profile:
Swastik Powertech is a forward-thinking energy solutions company dedicated to providing reliable, sustainable, and cost-effective solar power systems for both residential and commercial needs. As a sister concern of the well-established Swastik Distributors, which boasts over a decade of experience in the distribution of industrial transformers and motors, Swastik Powertech carries forward a strong legacy of technical expertise, trust, and customer satisfaction.
With a commitment to driving the transition towards clean energy, Swastik Powertech offers end-to-end solar solutions—from consultation and system design to installation and after sales service. Whether it's powering homes with rooftop solar panels or equipping businesses with large-scale commercial setups, we ensure top-quality products, seamless execution, and long-term support.
Our deep understanding of electrical systems, backed by the industrial strength of our parent company, positions us uniquely in the solar market. We aim not only to reduce energy costs for our clients but also to contribute meaningfully to a greener and more sustainable future.
At Swastik Powertech, we believe the future is solar—and we are here to power it.
